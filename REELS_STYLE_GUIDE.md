## 1. BRAND REFERENCE ANALYSIS

### A. Claude / Anthropic (@claudeai -- 639K followers, 208 posts)

**Brand Colors:**
- Primary: Warm rust-orange (#C15F3C, called "Crail")
- Secondary: Mint green, light purple, soft gradients
- Neutrals: Off-white and light-grey tones
- Notably absent: Deep blues

**Visual Aesthetic:**
- Minimalist, clean, rounded/humanistic feel
- Pastel tones with hand-drawn style illustrations
- Warm, friendly, approachable -- described as "Duolingo and Notion had a baby"
- Soft gradients rather than hard edges or neon
- Logo is an abstract starburst/pinwheel (ideas radiating outward)

**Design Philosophy:**
- "Timeless, thoughtful aesthetic" -- more academic think-tank than flashy tech startup
- Restraint and professionalism over flashiness
- Trust, transparency, human-centric approach
- Avoids over-the-top flourishes or hyper-futuristic fonts
- Calm professionalism that differentiates from competitors using neon gradients

**Content Approach:**
- Product feature highlights with clean UI demos
- Educational content about AI capabilities
- Conversational, approachable tone

### B. OpenAI / ChatGPT (@openai)

- Five weights: Light, Regular, Medium, Semibold, Bold (each with italic)

**Visual Identity:**
- "Blossom" logo with geometric principles
- The "Point" (black dot) and the "Bloom" are core design elements
- "Emotive Point" provides animated expression on digital platforms
- Commissioned human photography (landscapes, still life, abstract forms)
- AI-generated visuals from Sora used but human-curated

- "Surprisingly human" -- real people, authentic settings, genuine voices
- Product launches staged casually (living room setting, presenters chatting informally)
- September 2025 campaign: Real actors, real kitchens, real locations
- Super Bowl ad: Single black dot morphing through human history
- Emphasis on companionship for daily life over technical specs
- Intentionally uses human-created content (contrarian to AI-generated trend)

**Key Marketing Insight:**
"The brand most associated with artificial intelligence paradoxically markets itself as more human than most tech competitors."

## 2. RECOMMENDED RILEYAI REELS STYLE GUIDE

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Primary Background | Dark Navy/Black | #0D1117 or #111827 | Main background (dark mode feel) |
| Primary Accent | Riley Green | Pick from brand | Highlights, CTAs, key numbers |
| Secondary Accent | Electric Blue | #3B82F6 | Charts, data points, secondary elements |
| Positive/Gains | Bright Green | #22C55E | Stock gains, positive metrics |
| Negative/Losses | Soft Red | #EF4444 | Risk warnings (use sparingly) |
| Text Primary | White | #FFFFFF | Headlines, key stats |
| Text Secondary | Light Grey | #9CA3AF | Subtext, captions, labels |

**Rationale:** Dark backgrounds (like OpenAI's product UI and Robinhood's dark mode) convey sophistication and make financial data pop. Green accents tie to investing/growth.

---

### Typography Treatment

**Headlines/Key Stats:**
- Bold, sans-serif (Inter Bold, SF Pro Bold, or similar geometric sans)
- Large: 48-72px equivalent on 1080x1920 canvas
- White on dark background
- Appear with a quick fade-in or scale-up animation (0.3s)

**Body Text/Explanations:**
- Regular weight sans-serif
- 28-36px equivalent
- Light grey (#9CA3AF) or white at 80% opacity
- Appear line-by-line or word-by-word for pacing

**Numbers/Financial Data:**
- Extra bold or tabular numerals
- Accent color (green for gains)
- Scale up animation with a slight bounce
- Use $ and % symbols prominently

**Text Animation Patterns (for Creatomate):**
1. **Typewriter reveal** -- text types in character by character (for hooks)
2. **Fade-up** -- text slides up from below with opacity fade (for body)
3. **Pop-in** -- text scales from 0 to 100% with slight overshoot (for stats/numbers)
4. **Wipe reveal** -- text revealed left-to-right behind a colored bar (for emphasis)

---

### Duration and Pacing

**Optimal Length:** 15-30 seconds (sweet spot for algorithm + retention)

**Pacing Structure:**
| Segment | Duration | Content |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Hook | 0-2s | Bold statement, question, or surprising stat |
| Setup | 2-5s | Context/problem statement |
| Core Content | 5-20s | 2-4 key points, each on screen 3-4 seconds |
| CTA | 20-25s | "Download RileyAI" / "Link in bio" |
| Logo Hold | 25-27s | Brand lockup with tagline |

**Critical Rule:** First 1.7 seconds determine whether viewers stay. The hook must be visually bold and textually provocative.

**Text Timing:**
- Each text element stays on screen 2.5-4 seconds
- Never more than 2 lines of text visible simultaneously
- New text replaces old (don't accumulate -- keep it clean)

---

### Music and Audio

**Music Style:**
- Lo-fi beats, minimal electronic, or trending Reels audio
- Energy level: Medium (not overpowering, not sleepy)
- Fintech vibe: Clean, modern, slightly techy but warm

**Voiceover (Optional):**
- Natural, conversational tone (not corporate announcer)
- Male or female, mid-20s to mid-30s voice
- Pacing: ~150 words per minute
- Design for silent viewing FIRST (80% watch muted) -- captions are mandatory

**Audio Strategy:**
- Use trending audio when possible (massive algorithm boost)
- If using original audio, keep it minimal and let text carry the message
- Beat drops can sync with stat reveals for impact

---

### Branding Elements

**Logo Placement:**
- Small watermark in top-right or bottom-right corner (semi-transparent, 60-70% opacity)
- Full logo lockup only in final 2-3 seconds
- Never let the logo compete with content

**Brand Consistency:**
- Every reel should use the same dark background tone
- Accent color usage should be consistent (same green, same blue)
- Maintain consistent text positioning (centered or left-aligned, pick one)
- Use a consistent intro animation (e.g., RileyAI logo pulse into content)

**Recurring Visual Motifs:**
- Subtle grid lines or chart patterns in background (nods to trading)
- Gentle particle effects or floating dots (nods to AI, like OpenAI's "Point")
- Clean card-style containers for data/stats
- Minimal glow effects on key numbers

---

### Content Formats (Ranked by Viral Potential)

**Tier 1 -- Highest Share/Save Potential:**

1. **"Did You Know?" Financial Facts**
   - Hook: Shocking stat or counterintuitive fact
   - Format: Text on screen with minimal animation
   - Example: "The S&P 500 drops 10%+ once every 1.5 years. Here's why Riley keeps you invested."
   - Why it works: Curiosity gap + save-worthy information

2. **AI vs. Human Investor Comparison**
   - Hook: "I let AI manage $10K for 30 days..."
   - Format: Split screen or before/after with real portfolio data
   - Why it works: Transformation content + debate triggers DM shares

3. **Quick Tips / "3 Things" Lists**
   - Hook: "3 mistakes killing your portfolio"
   - Format: Numbered list, each point gets 3-4 seconds
   - Why it works: Structured, save-worthy, educational

**Tier 2 -- Strong Engagement:**

4. **Product Demo Moments**
   - Show the app doing something impressive in <15 seconds
   - Screen recording with zooms/highlights
   - Styled like OpenAI's casual product demos, not corporate

5. **Myth Busting**
   - Hook: "Stop dollar-cost averaging" (contrarian)
   - Format: Myth stated, then debunked with data
   - Why it works: Contrarian hooks get 5-10x more reach

6. **Market Moment Reactions**
   - Quick take on today's market move
   - Text overlay on dark background with relevant chart
   - Post within hours of the event for timeliness

**Tier 3 -- Brand Building:**

7. **Behind the Scenes / Team Content**
   - Authenticity signals (like OpenAI's human-first approach)
   - "How we built X feature" or team reacting to market moves

8. **User Testimonials / Results**
   - UGC-style, phone-recorded feel
   - Real numbers, real people (or text-based quotes)

---

### What Makes It Feel "Viral" vs. Generic

| Viral | Generic |
|-------|---------|
| Opens with a bold, specific claim | Opens with "Hey guys!" or logo intro |
| Dark, moody, premium color palette | Bright white corporate background |
| Text does the talking (designed for mute) | Relies entirely on voiceover |
| One idea per reel, executed cleanly | Tries to cover 5 topics in 30 seconds |
| Numbers and data front and center | Vague claims without proof |
| Ends with a reason to share/save | Ends with "Follow for more!" |
| Consistent visual brand every reel | Different style every post |
| Uses trending audio strategically | Uses generic stock music |
| Feels like a friend sharing a tip | Feels like a corporate ad |

## 3. CREATOMATE TEMPLATE SPECIFICATIONS

### Template 1: "Stat Hook"
```
Canvas: 1080x1920 (9:16)
Background: Solid #0D1117
Duration: 18 seconds

Layer 1 (0-18s): Background with subtle animated grid pattern
Layer 2 (0-2s): Hook text -- bold, 64px, white, center, typewriter animation
Layer 3 (2-5s): Context text -- 32px, grey, fade-up
Layer 4 (5-9s): Big number/stat -- 96px, green accent, pop-in with bounce
Layer 5 (9-14s): Explanation -- 28px, white, 2 lines max, fade-up
Layer 6 (14-16s): CTA text -- 36px, accent color, fade-in
Layer 7 (0-18s): Logo watermark -- top-right, 40px, 60% opacity
Layer 8 (16-18s): Full logo lockup -- center, fade-in
```

### Template 2: "3 Tips List"
```
Canvas: 1080x1920 (9:16)
Background: Gradient #0D1117 to #1a1a2e
Duration: 25 seconds

Layer 1 (0-2s): Hook -- "3 [topic]" bold text, pop-in
Layer 2 (2-9s): Tip 1 -- Number "1" in accent, text in white, slide-in from left
Layer 3 (9-16s): Tip 2 -- Same treatment
Layer 4 (16-22s): Tip 3 -- Same treatment
Layer 5 (22-25s): CTA + Logo lockup
Layer 6 (0-25s): Logo watermark
```

### Template 3: "Product Demo"
```
Canvas: 1080x1920 (9:16)
Background: #0D1117 with phone mockup frame
Duration: 20 seconds

Layer 1 (0-2s): Hook text above phone mockup
Layer 2 (2-16s): Screen recording inside phone frame, with zoom highlights
Layer 3 (2-16s): Callout bubbles pointing to features
Layer 4 (16-18s): Results/outcome text -- big number, green
Layer 5 (18-20s): CTA + Logo
```

### Template 4: "Market Moment"
```
Canvas: 1080x1920 (9:16)
Background: #0D1117
Duration: 15 seconds

Layer 1 (0-2s): "BREAKING" or ticker symbol -- bold red/white, shake animation
Layer 2 (2-6s): What happened -- 36px white text
Layer 3 (6-10s): What it means -- 28px grey text
Layer 4 (10-13s): "Riley's take" or what the AI did -- accent color
Layer 5 (13-15s): CTA + Logo
```

## 4. PRODUCTION CHECKLIST

For every reel before publishing:

- [ ] Hook is in the first 1.5 seconds (no logo intros, no "hello")
- [ ] Readable with sound OFF (all key info is in text)
- [ ] Maximum 2 lines of text visible at any time
- [ ] Dark background used consistently
- [ ] Brand accent colors match the palette above
- [ ] Logo watermark is present but not distracting
- [ ] Total duration is under 30 seconds
- [ ] Ends with a clear CTA (not just "follow for more")
- [ ] Caption includes 3-5 relevant hashtags + a hook sentence
- [ ] Trending audio used where appropriate

---

## 5. KEY ALGORITHM INSIGHTS (2026)

1. **Sends (DM shares) are the #1 signal** -- design content people want to forward to a friend
2. **Saves are worth 10x a like** -- make content reference-worthy (tips, stats, lists)
3. **70%+ watch time** is the target -- keep it short and punchy
4. **Original content gets 40-60% more distribution** than reposts
5. **Post 3-5 reels per week** minimum for algorithm traction
6. **Consistency beats virality** -- same visual style every post builds recognition
