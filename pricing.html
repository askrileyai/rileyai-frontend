Frequently Asked Questions

How do monthly hours work?

Hours track your active trading session time. When you're actively analyzing charts with Riley, the timer runs. Chat doesn't count against hours. Hours reset on the 1st of each month.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! Upgrade immediately to unlock features. Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle.

What happens if I run out of hours?

You can still chat with Riley, but live session tracking pauses until next month or you upgrade. No data is lost.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, 7-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, we use Stripe for payment processing. We never see or store your credit card information.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel anytime from your account settings. No long-term contracts.
